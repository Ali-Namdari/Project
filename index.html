Final Project by Ali Namdari


Rules of the game:

  • 1) If your second roll is a 1, you get a 0 for the round.
  • 2) If your second roll is the same as your first roll, then the second roll will be multiplied by the first roll to add to your total.
  • 3) If the rolls do not match the conditions above, then first and second roll are simply added together.
